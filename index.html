About

Ocean Knowledge at Voice of the Ocean aims to address critical questions relating to the biological, chemical and physical health of the Baltic Sea. By operating exciting, cutting edge technology at strategic sites, we are able to provide consistent oceanographic data freely to the researchers who can answer these questions.

Smart Autonomous Monitoring of the Baltic Sea

The project seeks to fundamentally change the landscape of ocean observation in the Baltic Sea by collecting the largest, and highest resolution, data set of the Baltic Sea, from chemistry and physics at the molecular level, to weather, fisheries and ocean functions across the Baltic Sea
